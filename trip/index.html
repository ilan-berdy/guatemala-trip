In or beside the village

Walkable to Tinker St. · minimal driving after you park

  • Stroll to coffee, dinner, and shops without a DD rotation
  • Great if you want late evenings in town and easy mornings
  • Often smaller or pricier per bedroom for peak summer weekends

A few minutes out

Short drive into town · often whole-house rentals

  • More space, yards, and parking for two cars — often better for four adults
  • Quieter nights; plan on driving for dinner or carpooling
  • Watch winter/spring mud season on mountain roads (June is usually fine)
